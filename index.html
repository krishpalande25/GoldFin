A gold loan is a type of secured loan where you pledge your gold jewelry or ornaments as collateral to borrow money. The loan amount depends on the purity and weight of your gold, typically up to 75% of its market value.

Our interest rates range from 12% to 16% per annum depending on the loan amount and tenure you choose. We offer competitive rates with transparent pricing and no hidden charges.

We provide approval within 24 hours of application submission and documentation verification. In many cases, you can receive funds the same day if all documents are in order.
